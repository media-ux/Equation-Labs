The problem

Business as usual burns money and carbon.

01

Landfills leak methane

Organic waste rotting in the open releases methane — a gas roughly 80× more potent than CO₂ over 20 years.

02

Incineration wastes the energy

Burning waste throws away its energy value and pushes carbon straight into the atmosphere.

03

Diesel keeps the lights on

Off-grid sites still lean on diesel generators — expensive, polluting, and exposed to fuel supply shocks.

What comes out

Three outputs from one machine.

Clean, reliable energy

Syngas drives generators for 24/7 electricity and heat — replacing diesel right at the source.

Carbon-negative biochar

Every cycle yields stable biochar that locks carbon in soil for centuries and restores fertility.

Less landfill, less methane

Waste is diverted and valorised on-site — cutting hauling, tipping fees and fugitive emissions.

About

We think waste is just energy in the wrong place.

Equation Labs is an engineering company building the gasification hardware that closes the loop — turning the world's hardest-to-handle waste into power and stable carbon.

Our mission

Decentralised, carbon-negative energy — anywhere.

A billion people live without reliable power, and the world buries or burns most of its waste. We saw one answer to both: small, modular gasifiers that run where the waste already is.

Our systems are built to be shipped, deployed and operated far from any grid — proving carbon-negative energy isn't a someday technology, but a working one.
